Attention! If you see red coloured text, it means that we will be creating an article for the link. However, it doesn't link to anything at the moment. Look forwards to seeing those soon. Thank you!

There is a massive spectrum of sexual and romantic orientations in the world. Generally, they are categorized under four umbrella terms: heterosexuality, homosexuality, bisexuality, and asexuality. However, there are many more labels that many people like to use. Whether they feel it fits them well, or just feel comfortable with it, the label that a person chooses is completely up to them. Some might prefer not to use one at all. Here you can learn about different sexual and romantic orientations, and what they mean.
