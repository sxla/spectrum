Gender

Attention! If you see red coloured text, it means that we will be creating an article for the link. However, it doesn't link to anything at the moment. Look forwards to seeing those soon. Thank you!

Gender is not only limited to a binary; binary is for code! Although biological sex is predetermined through chromosomes, that does not mean that gender is as well. Three common umbrella terms for gender include male, female, and genderqueer/non-binary. However, there are many more specific labels as well. Many of these may heavily overlap, but there are still differences. If a person chooses to use a certain label, it is their right to use it as long as it makes them feel comfortable.
