About Us

Spectrum is a website created by two KAS students, named Oriana C. Roth-Vinson and Stella S. Lee. We have the goal of creating a supportive environment for LGBTQ+ youths around the world, starting with our own school. We want to teach others about the LGBTQ+ community, and advocate for those who are oppressed. This website will be filled to the brim with informational articles that all can access and share.

What is Spectrum?

Spectrum is a website dedicated to teaching others about the LGBTQ+ community. As time passes, we will continue to add more informational articles about different sexual orientations, genders, and more. We hope that LGBTQ+ youths can be supported around the globe, starting with those in our own community.

We created this website with three goals in mind: To advocate for and support the LGBTQ+ community, to help others learn about the LGBTQ+ community, and to create a sense of community for those at our school and even others. We hope that with Spectrum, more people will be able to learn about LGBTQ+ issues, and realize that we are not so confusing or different at all. Afterall, we are all humans.

Why We Created Spectrum

This website started out as something that we had to do for school; an obligatory task. However, that quickly changed. We care a lot about the issues in our society, and wish for a world where acceptance is prevalent and bigotry is rare. We hope that our website will help others learn more about the LGBTQ+ community, and help others accept each other better.
