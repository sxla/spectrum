Welcome to Spectrum

Welcome to our website, Spectrum. We are a non-profit student organization that aims to to lessen the amount of bigotry in the world, starting from our school. Here you will be able to learn about the LGBTQ+ community.

More About Us.



Acceptance

There is a massive spectrum of sexualities in the world. In an ideal world, people would not face bigotry due to those that they are attracted (or not) to. We cannot do this overnight, but we can certainly take some small steps to achieve this goal. Go to our "sexuality" page for more information.

Fluidity

Gender is not only limited to a binary; binary is for code! Gender can be fluid, or it can be set in stone. It all depends on what you feel is the best for you. Transgender people have been shunned in society since a long time ago, but that too can change. Go to our "gender" page for more information.

Further

The LGBTQ+ community is not just limited to different sexualities and genders. It has a rich history, with many stuggles and triumphs. There are also many different terms that people have used and reclaimed, becoming a part of our identity. Go to our "more" page for more information.
